Cake recipe

Home Pasta Pizza Burger Cake Salad

Prep Time: 60 mins  

Difficulty: Medium

Ingredients

  • 2 Cups Flour
  • 1 cup sugar 
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 cup milk
  • Baking powder 

Instructions 

  1. Combine all the ingredients.
  2. Transfer to baking tray.
  3. 40 mins at 180°C /356°F baking.
  4. Cool and serve.
