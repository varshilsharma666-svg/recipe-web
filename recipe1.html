Pasta Recipe

Home Pasta Pizza Burger Cake Salad

Preparation Time: 30 mins

Difficulty: Simple

Ingredients

  • 200g pasta
  • 2 cups tomato sauce
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • Salt & pepper
  • Cheese

Instructions

  1. Boil pasta in salted water.
  2. Heat oil and add tomato sauce.
  3. Mix pasta with sauce.
  4. Add the cheese and serve hot.
